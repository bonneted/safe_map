How to Use This Tool

  • Choose Data Type: Select "Historical" for collected accident data or "Predicted" for risk assessment based on road properties.
  • Adjust Risk Coefficient: Use the slider to weigh the importance of past accidents or predicted risks in your route.
  • Keep Routes Alive: Enable this option to display multiple routes simultaneously.
  • Show Accident/Risk Data: Show a colormap of each road's past or predicted accidents.
  • Plotting a Route: Click on the map twice to set the starting and ending points for your route.
